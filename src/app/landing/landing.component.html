Northwestern's Open Textbook Library

Free Openly licensed Textbooks for Northwestern Students

Welcome to Northwestern's Open Textbook Library! This website is a collection of open textbooks for Northwestern students ran by ASG. If a professor has used an open textbook for a class, then search through our collection to find it. The mission of the Open Textbook Library is to provide students with a repository of courses with free textbooks that allow them to fulfill their major requirements at low cost.
